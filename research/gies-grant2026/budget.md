## Budget Justification

### 1. Research Assistants — $80,000

**PhD Research Assistant (1 RA, 50% appointment)**
- $24,000/year salary + ~$8,000 fringe benefits = $32,000/year
- Year 1-2: Full support for platform instrumentation, data collection management, qualitative coding, and co-authorship on Streams 1 and 2
- Year 3: Reduced to 25% ($16,000) as the RA transitions to dissertation work using platform data
- RA will be recruited from Business Administration, Information Sciences, or Education doctoral programs

**Undergraduate Research Assistants (2-3 per semester)**
- Funded through existing AgentLab stipend program (not charged to this grant)
- Contribute to platform development and testing under PI and PhD RA supervision

### 2. Software Development & Cloud Compute — $30,000

**Platform Infrastructure ($10,000/year Year 1, declining)**
- Cloud hosting for the research data collection platform (AWS/Vercel)
- LLM API credits for student project environments (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google — research tier pricing)
- Database hosting for interaction logging and research dataset (Neon Postgres)

**Research Software ($5,000 Year 1)**
- Process mining tools (ProM license, PM4Py enterprise support)
- Qualitative analysis software (NVivo or Atlas.ti, 2 licenses)
- Data pipeline and ETL tools for research dataset curation

Costs decline in Years 2-3 as infrastructure stabilizes and student projects partially offset compute through corporate challenge sponsorships (external to this grant).

### 3. Data Collection & IRB — $15,000

**IRB Protocol Development ($5,000 Year 1)**
- Consultant support for designing informed consent procedures for educational platform research
- Legal review of data retention and de-identification protocols

**Data Curation & Quality ($3,000-4,000/year)**
- De-identification pipeline development and validation
- Annotation scheme development and inter-rater reliability testing
- Dataset documentation following FAIR principles

### 4. Research Dissemination — $20,000

**Conference Travel ($5,000-8,000/year)**
- ICIS (International Conference on Information Systems) — primary venue for Stream 1
- AOM (Academy of Management) — primary venue for Stream 2
- AMCIS, HICSS, or Learning Analytics & Knowledge (LAK) — secondary venues

**Open Access Fees ($2,000-3,000 as needed)**
- Article processing charges for open-access publication of key findings

**NSF Proposal Preparation (Year 2-3)**
- No direct cost — PI time and RA support. Budget for any external reviewer or consultant fees for proposal strengthening.

### 5. Participant Compensation — $5,000

**Interview Participants (Stream 2)**
- 20-30 semi-structured interviews with faculty, staff, and administrators
- $50-$100 gift cards per interview participant
- Year 1: $3,000 (initial round), Year 2: $2,000 (follow-up interviews)

## Notes

- **Fringe benefits** are included in the RA salary line at the university's current graduate assistant rate (~25%).
- **No internal cost recovery overhead** is included per CFP guidelines.
