## Key Differences from Executive Summary

The [executive summary](../../docs/pitches/executive-summary.md) pitches the AI Innovation Engine as a strategic initiative to the Dean. This proposal reframes the same infrastructure as **research infrastructure** for the Wymer Research Fund:

- Leads with research questions, not operational flywheel
- Three research streams with named target journals
- Budget is 100% research spending (RAs, compute, data, dissemination)
- Emphasizes multi-investigator utility and open dataset
- Reduced ask ($150K vs $190K) — operational costs removed
