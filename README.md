# Gies AI Innovation Engine

Research and planning repository for building a multi-sided AI innovation platform at Gies College of Business, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

**[View the Executive Summary](https://vishalsachdev.github.io/ai-innovation-engine/)**

## What Is This?

The AI Innovation Engine is a platform where faculty, staff, corporate partners, and alumni post real-world AI challenges, and Gies students build solutions using shared open-source infrastructure. Every project grows a compound skill library, generates research data for studying human-AI collaboration, and feeds a startup commercialization pipeline.

The Engine coordinates infrastructure that already exists: the Magelli Office (200+ client projects/year), Origin Ventures ($5M endowment, iVenture Accelerator), ISOI research networks, Canvas MCP (90+ open-source tools), and 9,000+ students across on-campus and online programs.
